Global Financial Execution Ecosystem — Mantu
Product Owner 60+ countries 20+ subsidiaries Sovos · SEAM · Indicom · MISA · MagniFinance
Problem
60+ countries. 20+ subsidiaries. Fragmented e-invoicing across EU and Asia on a single shared ERP. Every country had different tax mandates, schemas, and compliance deadlines — with zero unified approach.
What I own
End-to-end Cash & Working Capital pillar: Cash Advance → Expense Declaration → Billing → Payment → GL entries. Architected central financial middleware connecting Expensya, TravelPerk, Kyriba, and the ERP simultaneously. Designed API flows and XML/JSON schemas per country gateway.
Outcome
100% tax compliance across all active markets. Multi-gateway e-invoicing live: Belgium/France (Sovos), China (SEAM), Italy (Indicom), Vietnam (MISA), Portugal (MagniFinance). ERP synchronization governing AR/AP lifecycle and audit traceability.
